Reports

There are three reports available.

  • All Payments
  • Who has paid
  • Who has not paid

All Payments

This report will show all the customers and their paid status based on the year given.

Select a year and click the "Generate" button

Who has paid

This report will show all the customer who have paid based on the month and year.

Select a month and a year, then click "Generate" to generate the report.

Who has not paid

This report will show all the customers who haven't paid based on the month and year.

Select a month and a year, then click "Generate" to generate the report.
