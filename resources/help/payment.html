Payments

To make payments:

  1. Click "File"
  2. Click "Payments"

OR

  1. Click the Dollar sign icon on the toolbar

The payment form will be displayed.

Choose a month, day, and a year. Click Submit when finished

All the customers who haven't ended their service will be displayed. There will be a check mark under the payment_date column if the customer has already paid. Type in the amount (if required), and put a check in the payment_date column for all the customers.

Click the "Save Changes" button when finished
